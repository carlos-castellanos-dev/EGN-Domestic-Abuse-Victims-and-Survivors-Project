Share Your Experience

Welcome to the Share Your Experience data entry form. Below we have provided a form to allow those who were or are victims of domestic abuse to share their experiences. We believe that by providing a means for victims to share their court professionals and outcomes will be an important first step to bringing some degree of transparency to the process and system, exposing patterns of disregard for abuse, evidence, and claims of abuse, which can be used to bring policy change, individual and/or group tort claims, censure, monetary deterrents, and overall reform.
